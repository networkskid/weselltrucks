About Our Business

WeSell Trucks was founded with one mission: to connect people with reliable, high-quality trucks at fair prices. What started as a small side hustle flipping used pickups out of a garage has grown into a trusted name in the truck market.

Our founder always had a passion for engines and entrepreneurship. In 2020, he bought and restored his first truck, sold it online, and reinvested the profit. Word of mouth spread quickly, and before long, WeSell Trucks was born.
